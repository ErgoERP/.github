# Ergo

### Lightweight ERP system, designed to serve small businesses
#### Developed by Mason Ritchason ([@masonritchason](https://github.com/masonritchason)).

**Ergo** aims to create a more _balanced_, weildy ERP system for businesses that don't quite meet the needs of a classic at-scale ERP.

> [!TIP]
> Ergo is in alpha development.
>
> While we work to build our platform, you can contact us to learn more. [@masonritchason](https://github.com/masonritchason)
> 
> See development roadmap [here](https://github.com/orgs/ERgoERP/projects/1/views/2).

### Platform
At the core, **Ergo** is powered by **Ergo Platform**, a central workspace for ERP processes. Classic, crucial enterprise planning workflows live here, available to all business users. 

**Ergo Flows**

Sales control with order management, customer relationship tools, and internal representative management.
At the front of Platform is a high quality sales funnel connection.
With or without Integrations, **Ergo** users can easily convert sales orders and information into production ready data.
Setting landmarks along the way, Sales teams can stay up-to-date with production processes, without the need for manual communications.

Production and process flow management, including advanced planning and scheduling, flow planning, and inventory management.
Fully integrated with both ends of the financial business, your Sales and Accounting teams will know what's up.
Manage your production and process flows with in-depth control structures.
Create process landmarks that can set off custom automations.
Manage active production orders all in one place, with powerful reporting tools.

Invoicing and accounting centers, which help improve conversion of business and customer relations.
Bringing up the tail of **Ergo's** feature stack is a robust accounting suite.
When production is complete, invoices and other accounting info can be automatically generated.
Close sales orders and collect on A/R accounts with ease.

### Fully Customized

**Ergo's** core functions are built around extensibility. Businesses choosing **Ergo** receive fully user-designed customization. Create, manipulate, and use ERP data however you choose.

### Communication-Focused

Manual communication pipelines are a pain-point for most small businesses. **Ergo** targets that struggle with robust automated communication.

Whether you prefer emails or in-platform notifications, **Ergo** can fire messages on nearly _any event_.

### Integrations
Don't uproot your whole business flow. We support several integration packages for common business applications.
